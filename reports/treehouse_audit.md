# WELCOME TO TREEHOUSE

### An all-day dining and beverage space designed especially for the vibrant,
hardworking folks of Sorrento Mesa, we offer a distinctly refined menu of
seasonal flavors to be enjoyed swiftly or slowly, for here or to-go.

# GOOD FOOD AND GOOD TIMES, FROM SUNRISE TO STARLIGHT

### United by one magnetic energy and a dedication to fresh, thoughtfully
crafted food, Treehouse offers three distinct spaces to match your mood. So
grab a quick latte, linger over lunch, or celebrate with friends- we’re here
for whatever your day brings.

Visit Treehouse for fast service and chef-crafted grab & go delights. Our
market counter is brimming with seasonal staples and curated pantry goods to
elevate your everyday eating.
